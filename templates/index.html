Instructions

  • There are markers for restaurants that appear when they're open, or else they don't appear.
  • The colored lines and markers represent parking zones that are currently enforced, or else you have the selected permit for:
    • Visitor lots are blue.
    • Red represents commuter students.
    • Light green represents residential students.
    • Dark green represents Walker residential students.
    • Staff and faculty parking is represented by purple.
    • Gated faculty parking is dark purple.
